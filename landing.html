Frequently Asked Questions

How does Notion Slides work?

Notion Slides reads the content of your Notion page directly in your browser and converts it into a presentation format. Headings become slide titles, text blocks become bullet points, and images are preserved. Your content never leaves your browser — we process everything locally for maximum privacy.

Do I need to have Notion open to use this?

Yes, Notion Slides works directly on your open Notion pages. Simply navigate to the Notion page you want to convert, click our extension icon, and we'll transform that page into a presentation.

Can I customize the presentation themes?

Absolutely! Free users can choose from our selection of basic themes. Pro and Team users get access to additional premium themes and customization options including custom colors, fonts, and layouts.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data never leaves your browser. We process everything locally and don't store or transmit your Notion content to any servers. Your presentations are stored in your browser's local storage and can be exported as needed.

Can I share my presentations with others?

For presenting live, you can simply share your screen like you would with any other presentation tool. However, we are working on an exporting features too.
