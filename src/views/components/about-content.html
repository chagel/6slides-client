About

Transform Your Notion Pages Into Elegant Presentations

Clean, concise, and completely private - your data never leaves your browser.

Notion to Slides is a Chrome extension that lets you present your Notion pages as elegant, minimalist slides, instantly transforming your documents into concise, impactful presentations. No complex tools or learning curve.

Features

100% Private & Secure
All processing happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
Instant Conversion
Transform your Notion pages into presentations with a single click.
Multiple Themes PRO
Choose from light and dark themes to match your presentation style.
Offline Usage
Present anywhere without internet once your page is loaded.
Focused Content
Lists, code blocks, tables, quotes, and images - all rendered with elegant simplicity.
Markdown Support PRO
Works with Markdown files and GitHub pages too, not just Notion.
Minimalistic Design
Clean typography and thoughtful whitespace create distraction-free presentations that emphasize your content.
Reliable Engineering
Built with robust, modern techniques to ensure smooth performance and compatibility with all Notion pages.
Upgrade to Pro

Unlock premium features like unlimited slides, multiple themes, and Markdown support
